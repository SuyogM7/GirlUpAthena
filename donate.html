Sanitary Napkin Donation Drive

Being a woman is a beautiful journey, but it encompasses a myriad of complexities. One of these complexities is menstruation, a monthly process in which a woman's body discharges blood and parts of the lining of the uterus monthly.

B&W Challenge

Webinar Gone Wrong, Or Did It?

Due to the sorry nature of sex education classes in schools, young children have taken to finding whatever information they can on the internet, through gossip, and from Wattpad fanfiction. However, we at Girl Up Athena believe that it is imperative that we are properly educated by experts when it comes to matters of our bodies, menstruation, hygiene and safe sex practices.
