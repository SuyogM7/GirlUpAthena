Who Are We?

GirlUp Athena comes under the GirlUp campaign, an initiative of the UN Foundations. We are a community that aspires to empower girls to become change-makers and the leaders of tomorrow as well as to raise awareness amongst others to help women around them! Our model revolves around achieving this throughout various donation drives, marathons, seminars, meet-and-greets, art exhibits, webinars and advocacy.
